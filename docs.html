How it works

AST-based scanning picks up mounts, routers, and handler bindings — not just string greps.

Collections

Organize requests the way your API is structured. Collections keep your workflows tight and replayable.

Organize

  • Nested folders with rename, duplicate, and delete
  • Auto-saved request history grouped by date

Test with environments

  • Global and collection-scoped environments
  • {"{{variable}}"} substitution across URLs, headers, params, and body
